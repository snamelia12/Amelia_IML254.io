My Hobbies

BADMINTON NETBALL EATING & TRYING NEW FOOD SLEEPING & HEALING AFTER SOCIAL INTERACTION
Badminton has always been more than just a sport to me—it’s my escape, my adrenaline rush, and my favorite way to stay active. I enjoy the fast-paced rallies, the sharp reflexes it demands, and the joy of smashing a shuttle across the court. Whether I’m playing competitively or casually with friends, badminton keeps me energized and motivated. Netball is a team sport that I’ve come to love for its dynamic pace and strategic gameplay. Playing netball has helped me improve my communication skills, quick decision-making, and overall fitness. Every match is a mix of fun, intensity, and learning, and it constantly pushes me to grow as both an individual and a team player. Exploring new food is one of my greatest joys. Whether it's local street eats or international cuisine, I love experiencing different flavors, textures, and cultures through food. I believe that every meal is an opportunity to discover something new and build unforgettable memories. As much as I enjoy engaging with others, I deeply value rest and solitude. Sleeping is not just a routine for me—it’s a way to reset, recharge, and care for my mental and physical health. Prioritizing sleep helps me stay grounded and ready to take on life with clarity and calmness.
